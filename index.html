Our Story

A Taste of Sicily in Northern Virginia

Giuseppe's Ristorante Italiano has been serving the Northern Virginia community for over 40 years. Formerly known as Joe's Pizza and Subs, we've grown into a full-service restaurant rooted in the traditions of authentic Sicilian cooking.

From our homemade pastas and hand-tossed pizzas to classic chicken marsala and frutti di mare, every dish is prepared with the care and pride of a family that loves to feed people well.

Whether you're joining us for a quiet weeknight dinner, a family celebration, or a catered special event — we are honored to share our table with you.
