Our Story

Since 1982, Giuseppe’s has brought authentic Sicilian flavors, warm hospitality, and family tradition to Haymarket.

A Family Tradition

Open Photo Album

Giuseppe’s was built on the idea that great food brings people together. From house-made sauces and fresh ingredients to timeless Sicilian recipes, every plate reflects decades of craft and care.

What started as a neighborhood favorite has become a gathering place for birthdays, celebrations, and everyday dinners with family and friends.

We’re proud to serve Haymarket with the same commitment to quality and hospitality that has guided us since day one.

— Giuseppe "Joe" Russo

Milestones

  • 1982 — Giuseppe’s opens in Haymarket, VA.
  • 1990s — Menu expands with signature pasta, seafood, and pizza favorites.
  • 2000s — Becomes a trusted destination for family dining and local celebrations.
  • Today — Continuing the tradition with authentic cuisine and heartfelt service.

Giuseppa "Josie" Russo — The Heart of Giuseppe's

Giuseppa "Josie" Russo was the heart and soul of Giuseppe's. Born in Italy and wed to Giuseppe for fifty beautiful years, Josie poured her love into everything she touched — from the cakes she baked by hand each morning to the warm smile and embrace she gave every guest who walked through our doors. Those who knew her remember a woman of extraordinary grace, quiet courage, and boundless generosity — someone who made you feel like family from the very first visit.
Josie will always be the heart of Giuseppe's.
